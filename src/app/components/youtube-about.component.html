About YoutubeK

Fork by Bernard Gabon base on angular2-yt-player of Alex Olari.

This is a simple music queing app for Youtube karaoke session, based on angular-cli 1.4, NWjs 0.25.2, Firebase/Firestore, ng2-youtube-player 0.0.3 (used for youtube iFrame API), SCSS (CSS3), HTML5 and webkit functions.

This karaoke player is compatible only with Chrome/webkit/Safari/Mac/iOS browsers.

The project status is beta.
